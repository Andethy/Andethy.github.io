Overview

Welcome to my discography, where I present my two distinct styles as a composer and electronic artist under the aliases Jack Hayley and Andethy. These dual profiles have allowed me to explore the vast and multifaceted world of music from two unique perspectives. In my composer alias, I delve into the realms of melody, harmony, and orchestration to create evocative and captivating musical compositions. Meanwhile, as Andethy, I venture into the electronic landscape, crafting innovative soundscapes that resonate with modern audiences. Through these two musical personas, I've had the privilege of producing a diverse body of work that showcases my versatility and dedication to the art of music creation.
